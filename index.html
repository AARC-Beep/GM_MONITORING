SeaCrew Dashboard — Daily Routine Log

Welcome — this user guide explains how to use the SeaCrew Dashboard.

  1. Login with your username & password (admin role can edit/delete entries).
  2. Use the sidebar to switch sections (Vessel Join, Arrivals, Updates, Memo, Training, P&I).
  3. Click Add to save an entry locally and send to the Google Sheet. Offline entries are re-tried automatically.
  4. To edit an entry, select the pencil icon and Update — edits are synchronized back to the sheet.
  5. Deleting an entry removes it locally and attempts to delete on the sheet (if UID exists).
  6. Chatboard posts will play a short bell when new messages are received.
  7. Monthly PDF: click Monthly PDF to generate a full monthly report (landscape, font 8) with signature block.

If you experience errors, run "Sync now" and check browser console for details. For support, contact IT.
